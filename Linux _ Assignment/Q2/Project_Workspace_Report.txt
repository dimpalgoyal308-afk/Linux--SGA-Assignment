    At first, the folder had 'drwxr-xr-x' permissions. This meant anyone on the system could look inside my files.

6 How these permissions protect project data:
The numbers '750' represent :
1. The first number (7) gives the Owner (me) full power to Read, Write, and change files.
2. The second number (5) allows my Group (team members) to look at and open files, but they cannot delete or change them.
3. The final number (0) completely blocks Everyone Else (others). Unauthorised users cannot see, open, or touch anything inside this project workspace, keeping our data completely secure.
